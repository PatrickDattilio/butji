# Suggested RSS Feeds for Butji.com News Section

Here are recommended RSS feeds to add to the news section, organized by category:

## AI & Tech News (General)

### Already Included in Seed:
- **TechCrunch AI**: `https://techcrunch.com/tag/artificial-intelligence/feed/`
- **The Verge AI**: `https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence/rss/index.xml`
- **Ars Technica AI**: `https://feeds.arstechnica.com/arstechnica/index/category/artificial-intelligence`

### Additional Recommended:
- **Wired AI**: `https://www.wired.com/feed/tag/artificial-intelligence/rss`
- **MIT Technology Review AI**: `https://www.technologyreview.com/feed/`
- **TechCrunch**: `https://techcrunch.com/feed/` (general tech, includes AI)
- **Reuters Technology**: `https://www.reutersagency.com/feed/?best-topics=business-finance&post_type=best`

## Layoffs & Tech Industry

- **Layoffs.fyi RSS**: `https://www.layoffs.fyi/feed/`
- **TechCrunch Startups**: `https://techcrunch.com/tag/startups/feed/`
- **The Information** (if you have access): Premium tech journalism, often has layoff coverage

## Critical/Critical Analysis of AI

- **The Markup**: `https://themarkup.org/feed.rss` (tech criticism, surveillance, AI)
- **Fast Company AI**: `https://www.fastcompany.com/ai/feed` (often includes critical takes)
- **The Guardian Technology**: `https://www.theguardian.com/technology/rss` (includes AI coverage with critical perspective)

## AI Companies & Big Tech

- **Bloomberg Technology** (if available): `https://www.bloomberg.com/feed/topics/technology`
- **CNBC Technology**: `https://www.cnbc.com/id/19854910/device/rss/rss.html`
- **Protocol**: `https://www.protocol.com/feed` (covers big tech companies)

## Alternative/Independent Tech Media

- **The Intercept Technology**: `https://theintercept.com/feed/?post_type=post`
- **ProPublica**: `https://www.propublica.org/feeds/propublica/main` (investigative journalism, sometimes covers tech/AI)

## How to Add Feeds

1. Go to `/admin/news-sources`
2. Click "Add News Source"
3. Enter the feed name and URL
4. Set type to "RSS Feed"
5. Enable the source
6. Click "Add Source"
7. Click "Fetch Now" to test and populate initial articles

## Notes

- Some feeds may require CORS headers or may not work from server-side
- Test each feed after adding to ensure it's accessible
- Some premium/publication feeds may have rate limits
- Consider adding filters later to focus on AI-related content if feeds are too broad

## Feeds to Avoid

- Avoid feeds from companies themselves (they'll be biased/PR-focused)
- Very broad tech feeds may have too much noise
- Paywalled content RSS feeds may not display full content
